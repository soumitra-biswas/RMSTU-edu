Basketball Club

About Us
Welcome to the Rangamati Science and Technology University Basketball Club! Our club is a vibrant community of basketball enthusiasts dedicated to promoting the sport, improving skills, and fostering a sense of teamwork and sportsmanship. Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, the Rangamati Science and Technology University Basketball Club is the perfect place for you.
Mission Statement
Our mission is to create a supportive and inclusive environment where students can enjoy basketball, develop their skills, participate in competitive play, and build lasting friendships. We strive to enhance the student experience through physical activity, sportsmanship, and community engagement.
Membership
Membership is open to all Rangamati Science and Technology University students, regardless of experience level. We also welcome faculty, staff, and alumni who are interested in participating. Our club prides itself on its inclusivity and community spirit..
